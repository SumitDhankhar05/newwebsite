Athletics & Sports Excellence

Nurturing physical health, collaborative sportsmanship, and competitive spirit. Explore our state-of-the-art sports facilities and training programs.

Infrastructure

World-Class Sports Facilities

We house advanced physical spaces designed for diverse disciplines, ensuring every student discovers and trains in their sport of choice.

Outdoor

Football & Athletics Stadium

Featuring a standard sized, well-maintained grass football field and an all-weather 400m synthetic running track. Equipped with high-illumination floodlights for evening athletic tournaments and regular practice sessions.

Indoor

Indoor Multi-Sports Arena

A temperature-controlled indoor hall equipped with three court spaces for Badminton (polished teakwood flooring), multiple Table Tennis tables, and high-impact safety nets. Perfect for round-the-year indoor sports development.

Outdoor

Basketball Courts Complex

Two synthetic outdoor basketball courts with non-slip, shock-absorbing flooring, acrylic backboards, and professional spring-action rings. The court complex features tiered spectator galleries for school league matches.

Indoor

Fitness & Gymnastics Zone

A specialized training room containing gymnastics vaulting horses, rings, safety landing mats, and strength training equipment. Managed by certified trainers to support athletic recovery and student fitness modules.

Development Path

Coaching & Training Programs

From introductory modules to competitive pre-national bootcamps, our sports curriculum supports all learning stages.

Curricular Physical Education

Weekly sports and wellness classes integrated into the timetable for Grades 1 to 12. Focuses on motor skills, general health awareness, yoga, and basic group team coordination.

Our Pride

Sports Achievements

Kendriya Vidyalaya AFS Suryalanka has a long history of competitive success in track, field, and team sports. Here are our recent major victories.

2025

KVS National Football Champions

Our Under-17 Boys Football team clinched the Gold Medal at the All-India Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan National Meet held in New Delhi, remaining undefeated throughout the tournament.

2024

SGFI State Track & Field Success

Vidyalaya athletes secured 2 Gold Medals (100m sprint, Triple Jump) and 3 Silver Medals at the School Games Federation of India (SGFI) State Athletics Meet, qualifying for the national team trials.

2023

Regional Basketball League Champions

Our Girls Senior Basketball team emerged as the champions of the KVS Chennai Region Basketball League, demonstrating outstanding teamwork, quick passing, and tactical play.

2022

Under-14 Chess Tournament Winner

Secured the 1st position in the Inter-School Chess Championship. Our young grandmasters played with sharp tactical foresight, showcasing intellectual excellence alongside physical sports.
