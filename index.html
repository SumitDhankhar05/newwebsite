Who We Are

Inspiring Excellence, Character, and Community

Founded on a vision of holistic development, PM Shri Kendriya Vidyalaya AFS Suryalanka merges advanced technological learning with foundational human values to shape tomorrow's innovators.

Academic Excellence

Our curriculum is designed to challenge students, spark curiosity, and foster deep conceptual understanding across sciences, arts, and humanities.

Holistic Growth

We emphasize emotional intelligence, physical fitness, creative arts, and leadership skills to cultivate well-rounded global citizens.

Global Perspective

We prepare students for a connected world by encouraging multicultural learning, bilingual skills, and community service awareness.

Academic Programs

A Roadmap to Lifelong Learning

Our comprehensive educational tracks cater to different developmental stages, ensuring academic enrichment for every student.

Primary School (Grades 1-5)

Building a strong foundation through playful discovery and structured inquiry.

Our primary program focuses on core numeracy, literacy, and introductory sciences. Through hands-on projects and interactive learning environments, we stimulate child development and teach collaborative values early on.

  • Inquiry-based learning models
  • Integrated visual and performing arts
  • Basic introduction to coding & computational thinking

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Strengthening analytical minds and expanding creative horizons.

Middle school serves as a transition where subjects become more specialized. We guide students to formulate opinions, evaluate data, and engage in critical discussions. STEM and humanities curricula are heavily integrated.

  • Advanced STEM programs & laboratory sessions
  • Structured debating and literary clubs
  • Comprehensive physical education & outdoor sports

High School (Grades 9-12)

Aspiring for global qualifications and leadership readiness.

We prepare high school students for college admissions and real-world challenges. Offering advanced placement programs, custom subject combinations, college counseling, and leadership opportunities.

  • Advanced Placement (AP) courses
  • Career counseling and university application guidance
  • Student Council and Model United Nations (MUN)

Admissions Process

Entering PM Shri Kendriya Vidyalaya is a simple, structured process designed to understand the student's unique potential.

1

Submit Inquiry

Fill out the online application query on our website below or download the application form from our portal.

2

Document Review

Our admissions board evaluates prior academic performance, teacher recommendations, and extracurricular sheets.

3

Interactive Session

A friendly interaction session with the applicant and parents to discuss interests, learning goals, and school alignment.

4

Admissions Offer

Successful candidates receive an invitation to enroll. Complete the documentation to secure your student's seat.
